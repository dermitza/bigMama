The bigMama constant current breakout

A simple breakout board for 16 NXP constant current sources PSSI2021SAY (PDF). The board dimensions are 25x50mm, ideal for Seeed Studio panelizing. The choice of resistors for each source is up to you, as is the color of the power LED (I prefer blue ;).
